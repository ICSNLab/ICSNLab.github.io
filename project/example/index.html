Quality of transmission estimation

Apr 27, 2022
Contact Us
Photo by Fraunhofer HHI on twitter

QoT estimation has always been a crucial task for optical communication systems, by predicting the transmission OSNR, BER or any other indicator of quality, the desiners could know whether the link is trustworthy or not in advance, they can take actions such as change the route or give higher system margin.

QoT estimation has been studied for years, however we are still facing some challenges. Modern optical networks are dynamic and running in ultra high speed, the conventional QoT estimation methods remains to be statical and lack of efficiency. Our research in QoT estimation tries to propose novel methods using the state-of-the-art model for addressing such challenges.
