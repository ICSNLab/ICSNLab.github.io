About ICSNLab

Intelligent Communication Systems and Networking Lab (ICSNLab) aims to propose competitive solutions for next-generation intelligent networks, specifically to solve challenging problems or explore useful phenomenons in ultra-fast optical transmission.

We tried to bring expertise in three main disciplines, i.e., machine learning (ML), fiber-optic communications and advanced digital signal processing together and lay out the steps to transform them into real impacts, and successfully developed advanced machine-learning-based signal processing techniques for future optical communication systems.

Now we work on a state-of-the-art fiber-optic communication system prototype for conducting extensive experiments. We welcome anyone interested in proposing new controlling algorithms/ signal processing algorithms/ high-performance devices for intelligent networks to contact us.
